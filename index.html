ABOUT WORX

WHO WE ARE

We are a community of women from different works of life blazing the trail with the use of Technology and digital skills. We have seen the light and so we encourage, educate, other women to embrace and use technology. technology is no longer an industry; [t is every industry.

Our Community

We connect you with over 1000 women from various works of life, background to learn, participate and grow your network.

Our Mission

To enlighten women of the need to embrace technology in their everyday in their everyday live through mentorship, coaching training, events and showcasing women making global impact with the use of technology and ICT.

Our Vision

A community of women from around the world bringing gender inequality gap in technology and using ICT tools to simplify our everyday lives.
